3. Data We Collect

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function (e.g., session management, CSRF protection, language preference, theme preference). These are stored in your browser's localStorage.

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage or using the cookie consent banner that appears on your first visit.
