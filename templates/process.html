How I Work



A Structured, Transparent Process



Every engagement follows a proven process designed to deliver results efficiently while keeping you informed and in control at every stage.


{{ t.hero.cta_primary }}
1

Discovery Call

We start with a free, no-obligation conversation. I want to understand your business, your data challenges, and what success looks like for you. This call typically lasts 30-45 minutes and helps us both decide if there's a good fit.

  • Understand your current data landscape
  • Identify pain points and opportunities
  • Discuss high-level goals and constraints
  • Answer any questions about my approach

Want to get a sense of pricing first? Try the Project Estimator — takes under a minute.

2

Scoping & Proposal

Based on our discovery call, I prepare a detailed proposal that outlines exactly what I'll deliver, how long it will take, and what it will cost. No surprises, no hidden fees. You'll know exactly what you're getting before we start.

  • Detailed scope of work with deliverables
  • Realistic timeline with milestones
  • Transparent pricing (fixed or time-based)
  • Technical approach and tool selection
3

Kickoff

Once you approve the proposal, we align on the practical details. I'll need access to your systems and data, and we'll agree on how we communicate, how often we check in, and who the key contacts are on your side.

  • Set up system access and environments
  • Agree on communication cadence (weekly calls, Slack, email)
  • Identify stakeholders and decision-makers
  • Confirm the project timeline
4

Delivery

This is where the work happens. I build iteratively, sharing progress regularly so you can give feedback early and often. No disappearing for weeks -- you'll see tangible results at each milestone and can steer the direction as needed.

  • Iterative development with regular demos
  • Weekly progress updates and check-ins
  • Early feedback loops to avoid rework
  • Documentation created alongside the solution
5

Handoff & Training

I don't just build and walk away. Every engagement includes knowledge transfer so your team understands the solution and can maintain it independently. You'll receive documentation, a walkthrough session, and hands-on training as needed.

  • Comprehensive documentation of the solution
  • Live walkthrough and Q&A sessions
  • Hands-on training for your team
  • Deployment to production with validation
6

Ongoing Support

After handoff, I'm still available. Whether you need a monthly retainer for continuous optimization, ad-hoc help when something breaks, or quarterly reviews to plan next steps -- I'm here for the long term.

  • Optional monthly support retainer (from CHF 500/month)
  • Performance monitoring and optimization
  • Quarterly strategic reviews
  • Priority support for urgent issues
