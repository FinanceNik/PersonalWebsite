The Challenge



A growing retail company with 50+ stores was drowning in data silos. Point-of-sale data, inventory systems, e-commerce platforms, and CRM tools all operated independently. The analytics team spent most of their time extracting and reconciling data rather than generating insights. They needed a unified platform that could handle their scale and grow with the business.

The Solution



I implemented a Microsoft Fabric Lakehouse architecture that centralized data from all source systems into OneLake. PySpark notebooks handled the heavy ETL processing -- ingesting millions of daily transactions, applying business rules, and building curated analytical tables. The medallion architecture (bronze/silver/gold layers) ensured data quality at every stage. Power BI reports using Direct Lake mode provided near real-time dashboards for store managers and executives alike.

Business Impact



60% Faster

Time-to-insight reduced from days to hours with automated pipelines and Direct Lake reporting.

Unified Data

5 previously siloed systems consolidated into one governed, scalable Lakehouse platform.

Self-Service Analytics

Store managers gained direct access to their performance data without IT bottlenecks.
