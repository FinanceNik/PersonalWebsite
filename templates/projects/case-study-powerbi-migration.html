The Challenge



A mid-sized financial services company was struggling with a fragmented reporting landscape. Over 50 Excel-based reports were being manually maintained by different departments, leading to inconsistent numbers, duplicated effort, and delayed decision-making. Leadership had no single source of truth for key financial metrics.

The Solution



I designed and implemented a centralized Power BI reporting platform backed by Azure Synapse Analytics. The project involved consolidating data from 8 source systems into a unified data warehouse, building a semantic layer with standardized financial measures, and deploying 15 interactive dashboards across the organization. Row-level security ensured each department only saw their relevant data, while scheduled refreshes eliminated manual data preparation.

Business Impact



80% Reduction

In manual reporting effort -- from 40+ hours/week across teams to under 8 hours.

Single Source of Truth

Eliminated conflicting numbers across departments with one unified data model.

Real-Time Insights

Leadership dashboard refreshed automatically, replacing monthly static PDF reports.
