Duolingo Analysis Tool


Data Analysis / Data Visualization


Technology Used: ⚡ Python | ⚡ Google Cloud Deploy | ⚡ Data Analysis

The Problem:

This application was born out of the idea that everyone should have access to meaningful insights about their fitness activities without any financial barrier. Many fitness enthusiasts use platforms like Strava, but the detailed analysis of your data often comes with a price tag.

The Solution:

The mission is to bridge this gap and make fitness data accessible to everyone. I believe that understanding your activity patterns, setting goals, and tracking your progress should be available to all, regardless of subscription plans. That's why we created this app – to empower individuals on their fitness journey. Analyzing your fitness data can be a powerful motivator. It not only helps you understand your achievements but also encourages you to push your limits. By providing insights into your activities, we aim to inspire you to take on new challenges, set personal records, and embrace a healthier lifestyle.

Key Features:

The dashboard has a file upload feature. With that every user can upload their own Strava data and get a personalized dashboard of their own. The dashboard is fully dynamic and automatically updates with every new file upload. Furthermore, the user can choose between different types of sports to analyze or analyze all sports types at once.

Summary:

All in all, this projects bridged the gap between a problem that exists for many users doing sports and tracking their corresponding data and eliminates the need for users to subscribe to expensive sports apps in order to basic analysis with their own data.
