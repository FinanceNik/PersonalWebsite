Frequently Asked Questions



How do I engage your consulting services?

Simply fill out the contact form on my website or send me an email. I'll schedule a free initial consultation to understand your requirements, and then provide a tailored proposal with scope, timeline, and pricing.

What industries do you work with?

I work across a wide range of industries including financial services, retail, manufacturing, healthcare, and logistics. The principles of good data architecture and reporting apply universally, and I adapt my approach to each industry's specific needs and regulations.

What tools and technologies do you specialize in?

My core specializations are Microsoft Fabric and Power BI. I'm also experienced with Azure Data Factory, Azure Synapse Analytics, SQL Server, Python, and the broader Microsoft data stack. I choose the right tools based on your specific requirements.

Do you offer remote consulting?

Yes, I work with clients both on-site and remotely. Most of my engagements are conducted remotely using screen sharing and collaboration tools, though I'm available for on-site workshops and meetings in the Luzern/Zurich area and can travel as needed.

What is a typical project timeline?

Timelines vary based on project complexity. A focused Power BI dashboard project typically takes 2-4 weeks. A full Microsoft Fabric implementation can range from 1-3 months. I provide detailed timelines during our initial scoping conversation.

Do you provide training and knowledge transfer?

Absolutely. Knowledge transfer is a key part of every engagement. I believe in empowering your team to maintain and extend the solutions I build. I also offer dedicated training workshops as a standalone service.

How do you ensure data security and compliance?

Data security is a top priority. I implement row-level security, follow the principle of least privilege, and ensure all solutions comply with relevant regulations such as GDPR. I can provide a detailed data protection plan tailored to your project.

Can you work with our existing Power BI reports?

Yes. I frequently help organizations optimize, refactor, or extend existing Power BI deployments. Whether you need performance improvements, a redesign, or migration to a new data model, I can work with what you already have.

What is your pricing model?

I offer both fixed-price project engagements and time-based consulting rates. Fixed pricing works well for clearly scoped projects, while hourly or daily rates are better for advisory work and ongoing support. I provide transparent quotes after our initial consultation.

Do you offer ongoing support contracts?

Yes. I offer monthly retainer packages starting at CHF 500/month that include monitoring, maintenance, bug fixes, and priority support. This ensures your data platform stays healthy and evolves with your business needs.
